The Forgotten Island

Synopsis?

This is the tricky part... The original idea (had back in 2017 at my sister's wedding) was this: a family was caught and cast out of society into the Pariah Zone. Before the family was caught, the mother gave her son secretly to a friend to take care of and raise. Unbeknownst to him, the boy has unparalleled magical powers. Join him as he discovers his powers, his past, and the plot to overthrow an empire eons old.

I don't know if the book will follow that exact premise, but that's the gist. A corrupt government who creates outcast scapegoats will be overthrown by a seeming nobody with immense power & potential. Oh, did I mention the government ruler has this same power & potential and has been secretly committing genocide to prevent its widespread use that could threaten his throne?? Yeah, it's going to be wild.

ETA?

With school and work and family taking up a lot of my time, I don't know when I will actually get around to writing the book. The time I spend now consists of creating the world map, outlining the general plot, and creating characters (scripting them, naming them, visualizing them). I'm hoping to have a rough draft done by 2027, and this might end up as a 3-4 book series if I can have my way with it.

What's been done so far?

As I mentioned, not a lot of time is able to be devoted to one of my lifetime goals of writing a novel/series yet, so these are the minor things that have been completed so far.

  • Map Creation - General lands & terrains
  • Outlining the Plot
  • Creating a world - outlining societal roles & functions, government structure,
  • Magic system(s) (there is likely going to be more than 1 at play) - functions, uses, and how to obtain & increase skills
